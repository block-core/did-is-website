So you want to create your own identity? Then there are a few steps you need to follow.

Publishing your identities is free, you don't need to pay anything.


Get the software

First you need to download and install Smart City Platform. Smart City Platform is a Web5 and blockchain software that includes functionality to manage your identities and also function as a cryptocurrency wallet.

Download link here: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/smart-city-platform/bmmhjnaohafphhlecmkkiaaglcebcapn


Create the account

After you download and install, you will create a new wallet. During this process, you will see 12-words. These are the key to your identities. Keep it private and never share with anyone.

If you ever loose your recovery phrase (12-words), you also loose access to edit your identities forever. Keep it safe, keep it hidden.

We do not have a backup, we do not have access to edit your identity if you loose your account.


Create your first identity

If you accept the default options when you create a new wallet, you'll automatically get an account named 'City Chain Identity'.

Open your 'City Chain Identity' and you'll see that it is currently not public (published). If you want to share it publicly with others, you can click the `Publish` button to send it to a server. You can't revert this operation, after you have made your identity known publicly, it is public forever.

You can always create additional identities within the same wallet by adding additional accounts of the type `Identity` with different account index.


Create a profile for your identity
